QUICK START
================================================================================

1. SETUP:
   Linux/macOS:  ./setup.sh
   Windows:      setup.bat

2. CONFIGURE:
   - Copy .env.example to .env
   - Add your YANDEX_TOKEN

3. RUN:
   Linux/macOS:  ./run.sh
   Windows:      run.bat

4. OPEN:
   http://localhost:5000

5. USE:
   - Select microphone
   - Click "Start Calibration" and clap 3-4 times
   - Click "Apply Suggested Threshold"
   - Click "Start Listening"
